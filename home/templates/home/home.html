Please follow the below instructions to scrape a business card
1. Click on the link "Upload Business Card" to upload a business card image(JPEG/PNG).
2. Naviagte to "Process Business Card" to process or delete the uploaded card.
3. Click on Process Card button to extract text from the card.
4. Processing the bsuiness card may take some time. Do not refresh the page!
5. Verify the fields such as Name, Org, Phone Number and Email.
    These fields can be corrected manually if the system fails to classify them correctly.
6. Click on "Save Contact" button to save the contact.
7. Navigate to "View Contact" to view/search a contact.
